About

The Poolesville Math Tournament (PVMT) is ran by dedicated Poolesville High School students from the Poolesville Math Team. We invite middle school students of all backgrounds and skill levels to participate!

This contest is open to all 6th-9th graders in the country. Note that all members on a team must be in the same division.

Students will compete in teams of at most 4 people. Smaller teams (of 1, 2, and 3 people) are also allowed, but note that these teams will be at a disadvantage for the team rounds because they have fewer people. There is no limit to how many teams a particular school/organization may register.


Format

Competitors will take three subject tests and a team round. The four subject tests available are Algebra/Number Theory, Geometry, and Combinatorics, and each subject test will be 45 minutes and consist of 10 problems. The team round is 45 minutes and consists of 20 problems.


Schedule

The current date for the competition is June 23, 2024. More information about the schedule will be released closer to the date of the competition.
